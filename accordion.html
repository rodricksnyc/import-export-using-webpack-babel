How do I submit a news item for WesInfo?

Use the online form to submit a news item. You will need to provide several pieces of information, including the headline and text for the news item and the dates it should appear. Also, be sure the information you provide is accurate and timely.


What is the purpose of the WesInfo home page News section?

The News section helps make WesInfo a useful and current information source for employees. The News section was developed in response to feedback from WesInfo users.


How do I submit a news item that needs to be posted immediately?

If your news item is time sensitive and requires immediate review and approval, complete the online form and select the High Priority button. You will be notified as soon as possible. If you need to discuss your request in more detail, contact the Information Resources' Information Desk (301-315-5903).


What kinds of news items are appropriate to post in the News section?

News should relate to Westat's business and research interests and operations, including the following: administrative and support services updates, technology updates, and information about staff and projects. Generally, news items should be of companywide interest or pertinent to a large Westat community (e.g., research staff). For examples of acceptable news items, browse through the WesInfo News Archives to see examples of news items posted previously.


Can personal ads be posted on WesInfo?

Personal ads (e.g., items for sale) and announcements (e.g., employee birthdays) will not be posted on WesInfo. Kitchen bulletin boards may be used for posting such information.

 

How long should the news item be?

  • A news item may be as brief as a headline and one sentence. It may be as long as is necessary.
  • News items longer than several paragraphs may require that a separate WesInfo page be created for it, if the full item overwhelms the News page.
  • Long items still will require a headline and a shorter version to appear on the News home page. http://wesinfo/NewsPage.cfm
  • Before these longer news items can be posted, the full text must be converted to WesInfo format. You will be contacted about the estimated turnaround time and/or when the page is ready for your review.

Will my news item be reviewed before posting?

After you submit the online form, your news item will be reviewed and approved by Lisa Bonaparte, the WesInfo News Administrator. It may also be forwarded to the Editorial Department to be proofread and edited lightly before posting. You will be contacted if significant revisions are required or if your request does not meet the guidelines.


For how many days is a news item displayed?

Generally, news items are posted on the home page for 2 weeks; however, the length of time may vary depending on the content and purpose. You are asked to indicate the start and end dates and times on the online form. News items will also appear in the news archive after they are removed from the home page.

 

Questions? Contact Lisa Bonaparte(email link), the WesInfo News Administrator.
